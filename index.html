Vision

The Future is Efficient

Software development is on the cusp of a massive transformation, driven by Generative AI. For too long, we've battled tool complexity and process friction. The future belongs to teams who solve business problems directly.

Imagine a plant manager capturing shop floor processes via video. Within hours, AI assists in creating a functional Digital Twin MVP, ready for feedback. This isn't science fiction; it's the efficiency AI enables. Developers, architects, and testers will focus on higher-value tasks: deep customer understanding, creative problem-solving, complex domain logic, and strategic thinking.

Agilp's vision is to help teams embrace this future – becoming faster, smarter, and more focused on delivering tangible business value. Let's fix business problems, not tool problems.

Mission

Driving Agile Efficiency

AGILP's mission is to empower organizations and professionals to navigate the evolving tech landscape with agility, efficiency, and foresight. Drawing on two decades of architecture experience (Cloud, Data, IoT, AI), Amitabh has seen firsthand how inefficiency and unclear requirements lead to waste.

As an Agile Coach, Amitabh's mission is to:

  • Instill True Agility: Foster the mindset for rapid iteration and adaptation.
  • Champion Simplicity: Cut complexity, advocate lean architectures, fight tech debt.
  • Sharpen Requirements: Ensure clarity (DoR/DoD/BDD) linked to business outcomes (OKRs).
  • Leverage AI Strategically: Help teams utilize AI/GenAI to automate and accelerate.
  • Amplify Voice: Ensure valuable insights are heard and acted upon.

The goal is to help you deliver successful, cost-effective solutions faster.

About

Amitabh R Jha

With over 25 years shaping complex systems as a Solution Architect (Cloud, Data, IoT, AI, Autonomous Driving, Industry 4.0), Amitabh has been at the forefront of tech innovation. However, witnessing repeated project struggles due to inefficiency and unheard technical concerns fueled his transition to an Agile Coach in early 2024 and founded AGILP.

AGILP embodies his approach: combining deep Agile principles with practical Help grounded in architectural reality, while embracing the potential of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). He believes in building solutions that are simple, efficient, and value-focused.

Certified SAFe 6 Practitioner, Azure Solutions Architect Expert, TOGAF Certified, MBA (MIS & Marketing). Innovator, published author, keynote speaker, mindfulness trainer committed to making a tangible difference.
